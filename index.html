Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and community to our neighborhood by hosting a free hot dog party. We believe that food is a universal language that can bring people together, and we want to create a fun and welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy delicious hot dogs and connect with their neighbors. Our party is open to people of all ages and backgrounds, and we aim to foster a sense of inclusivity and belonging. By providing a free meal and a space for socializing, we hope to promote a positive and friendly atmosphere in our community. We are committed to making this event a success and look forward to sharing the joy of free hot dogs with our neighbors.
